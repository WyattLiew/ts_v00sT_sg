FAQ
1. How does VÖOST Effervescent differ from other Supplement Brands?

    - Unlike many other effervescent brands, VÖOST has a wide range of vitamins that offers many different benefits. On top, it’s a great tasting vitamin that aims to help you stay boosted for your daily life.

2. Where is VÖOST Effervescent manufactured?

    - Our VÖOST Effervescent ranges are formulated in Australia and are manufactured in Germany

3. What are the VÖOST Effervescent range available in Singapore?

    - We currently offer 7 Vitamin and Mineral products providing different nutritional benefits. More product innovations are coming on the way.

4. Is VÖOST suitable for diabetic?

    - We do not add sugar in VÖOST effervescent tablets. We use Australian regulated and approved artificial sweetener to provide sweetness with little or no energy/kilojoules. We recommended you consulting with Healthcare Professional with any additional questions or concerns.

5. Can I take VÖOST Effervescent tablets if I have a medical condition?

    - We recommend you talk to your health professional before taking any vitamins or mineral supplements. They have a better understanding of your medical history and needs

6. Do the VÖOST Effervescent tablets contain preservatives?

    - We are pleased to advise we do not add preservatives in the VÖOST Effervescent Tablets.

7. Is VÖOST suitable for vegans & vegetarians?

    - VÖOST Magnesium, Energy, Vitamin B+ Performance, Mega B Complex, Immunity & Vitamin C Effervescent tablets are suitable for vegans and vegetarians.

    - VÖOST Multivitamin and Collagen Formation are the only products in the range which are NOT suitable for vegans and vegetarians.

8. How should I store my VÖOST Effervescent tablets?

    - We recommend the effervescent tablets are stored below 25⁰C in a cool, dry place.
